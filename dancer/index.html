Martin has danced with both the Vienna State Ballet and the Bavarian State Ballet performing a myriad of classical and contemporary pieces. He performed a soloist role in Romeo and Juliet and multiple demi-soloist roles in works by John Neumier, Merce Cunningham, Sir Fredrick Ashton and others.
